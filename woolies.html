Woolworths

For Goodwood shoppers, quality and convenience is now a short drive away! The Woolworths at N1 City Mall offers fresh fruit & vegetables, a butchery, bakery, groceries, fashion and homeware goods – all topped with the great service that consumers have grown to expect from one of South Africa’s most loved department stores.


So whether you’re in the mood for a quick snack and a coffee or a full monthly grocery shop, Woolworth’s convenience is simply unmatched.


This large Woolworths store is situated on the north side of the shopping centre near the American Swiss, Torga Optical and Edgars stores. Pine City Mall offers ample free parking and quick access to so many more stores, it certainly has become the Woolworths of choice for Pine City shoppers.
