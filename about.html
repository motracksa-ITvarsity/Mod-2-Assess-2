ABOUT US




In the heart of the Mother City, Pine City Mall has been a key part of everyday life in Cape Town since 1979.
Built on the site of the original train station, the centre forms an integral link between the CBD and its major public transport systems.
At Pine City Mall, the convenient location in the City Bowl brings you an exciting mix of food, fashion, services, cinema and essential stores.
Whether you’re on the hunt for the latest fashion trends, hanging out with friends or family, or making a quick stop on your daily commute, you can find everything you’re looking for under one roof.
