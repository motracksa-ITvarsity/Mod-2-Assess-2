Pick n Pay

One of South Africa’s most trusted and oldest grocery chain stores, Pick n Pay offers all grocery necessities- fruits, vegetables, pantry essentials, something for that sweet tooth and even clothing. This spacious shop offers low prices for quality items and promises great service.


Pick n Pay at Pine City Mall is situated close to the Wimpy, where you can find something delicious to eat before or after your shopping experience. It is also nearby to the public bathrooms and parking area.


Whether you’re looking to do your monthly grocery shopping, grab a few household items for the weekend or spoil yourself with a chocolate, Pick n Pay at Pine City Mall is your go-to store.
